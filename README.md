# Analytics Portfolio

Welcome to my data analytics repository! This space serves as a personal portfolio showcasing a variety of end-to-end data projects, focusing on product metrics, statistical analysis, and data-driven decision-making. 

Here, I apply analytical frameworks to solve business challenges, optimize product performance, and extract actionable insights from data.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack & Toolkit

### **Data Analysis & Scripting**
* **Python:** Advanced data manipulation, cleaning, and computation using **Pandas** and **NumPy**.
* **Statistical Analysis:** Hypothesis testing and significance calculations (e.g., **Z-test**, **Chi-square**) using **SciPy** and **Statsmodels**.

### **Data Warehousing & SQL**
* **SQL & BigQuery:** Writing optimized queries, aggregating large datasets, and managing cloud-based data warehouses for deep-dive analysis.

### **Business Intelligence & Visualization**
* **Tableau:** Building comprehensive, interactive analytics dashboards to track complex product performance and business KPIs.
* **Looker Studio:** Developing automated reporting and operational dashboards for quick business insights.
* **Python Libraries:** Static and dynamic plotting with **Matplotlib** and **Seaborn**.

---

## 🚀 Key Methodologies Applied
* **A/B Testing Workflow:** Practical execution of product A/B tests, checking metric distributions, and assessing statistical significance (Z-test, Chi-square) for business KPIs.
* **Product Metrics Analysis:** Defining and tracking core product frameworks, evaluating user behavior, cohort analysis, and funnel performance (Conversion, Retention).
* **Data Storytelling:** Translating raw database records into clear, actionable executive summaries and visual dashboards.
