# Email Metrics & Geographic Performance Dashboard

**Project Overview:**
This interactive Tableau dashboard focuses on tracking email marketing efficiency over time and analyzing performance variation across different geographic regions. It provides actionable insights into campaign health, user engagement trends, and regional benchmarks.

**Key Features:**
* **Email Metrics Dynamics:** Time-series analysis of core messaging KPIs (Open Rate, Click-Through Rate, Unsubscribe Rate) to monitor trends and performance shifts.
* **Geographic Performance:** Regional breakdown showing how different countries perform and interact with email campaigns.
* **Volume Tracking:** High-level overview of total emails sent, delivered, and overall audience growth by country.
